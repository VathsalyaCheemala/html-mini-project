ABOUT Vathsalya'S CAFE

Vathsalya's Cafe was started in 2020 by Mrs. Vathsalya in the heart of Hyderabad. With a deep passion for Indian street food and traditional flavors, the cafe blends classic recipes with a modern twist, creating a unique experience for all visitors.

We serve authentic South Indian breakfasts, flavorful biryanis, chaat items, and a wide range of Indian beverages. Every dish is made fresh with locally sourced ingredients and lots of love.

"Swad ka raaz dil se bana khaana." (The secret of taste is food made from the heart.)

Chef & Founder: Vathsalya

Opening hours: Open daily from 7am to 11pm

Address: 32 Madhapur, Hyderabad, Telangana - 500034
